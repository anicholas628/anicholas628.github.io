About Me

I have developed a beautiful love and passion for the art that is coding. I love producing words on the screen and watching them come to life when I open my browser. Coding is a puzzle game. I often find myself asking the question, "Why isn't this working?" I become a detective, solving my coding puzzles each day as I work on projects. Art and puzzles. I have become somewhat of an addict to code.

So what can I do? All of my projects were created using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. My first experience with a framework after learning those three was Bootstrap. I've also dabbled with APIs with JSON responses and Github. Here's my experience so far:
